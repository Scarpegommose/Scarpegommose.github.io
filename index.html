Bubbles: A Free Visual Timer Designed for ADHD Time Blindness

If you have ADHD, "I must finish this task in 15 minutes" means absolutely nothing. This is what "time blindness" means.
Bubbles is designed to turn time into something you can easily visualize.
You create bubbles with a set duration, and they gradually become smaller to let you understand at a glance how much time you have left.
Suddenly, time starts making sense.
"I must finish this task before the bubble pops" may seem like a small difference to you, but to your brain it's like day and night.

Bubbles: Visual Timer is entirely free. You can download it from the Google Play Store
